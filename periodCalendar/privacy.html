Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information in our mobile application Period Calendar.

1. Information We Collect

  • Health and Cycle Data: You may enter personal information like period dates, symptoms, mood, etc. This is stored locally on your device unless explicitly backed up by you.
  • Device Info: We may collect anonymized device data (like OS version, app version) for crash reporting and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To help you track and understand your menstrual cycle.
  • To improve the app experience and reliability through analytics and crash reports.
  • To send notifications based on your cycle (if enabled).

3. Data Security

All data is stored securely. No health data is shared with third parties without your consent. Optional features like cloud backup or analytics are opt-in and encrypted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications. These services may collect basic device data in compliance with their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You may delete your local data anytime via the app. If cloud backup is enabled, you can also request complete deletion by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at contact@yourapp.com

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted in this section.
